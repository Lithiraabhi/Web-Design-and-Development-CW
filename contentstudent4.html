Steps To Protect

1. Establish Marine Protected Areas (MPAs)

Governments and conservation organizations have the authority to designate specific areas in oceans and seas as MPAs where human activities such as fishing, mining, and shipping are either restricted or prohibited. This action helps conserve marine biodiversity and habitats.

2. Promote Sustainable Fishing Practices

Encourage the adoption of sustainable fishing practices such as implementing quotas, regulating fishing gear to minimize by catch, and promoting responsible aquaculture. This ensures that fish populations are not overexploited, preserving marine ecosystems

3. Combat Pollution

Our organization work to reduce pollution entering marine environments by advocating for stricter regulations on industrial waste discharge, promoting recycling and waste management practices
